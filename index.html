Not ice cream nor crashed ice: it's the unique Sicilian Granita!

posted on 25 May 2021

"Although its texture varies from coarse to smooth, it is always different from that of ice cream, which is creamier, and from that of sorbet, which is more compact; this makes Granita distinct and unique." (Granita-Wikipedia).

Have your Granita for breakfast, almond and coffee, very common choice in the east coast of Sicily, or pistacho, chocolate, lemon or black mulberry flavoured or as a refreshing mid morning snack, after sunbathing in hot Sicilian summer, dipping in it your Brioche (click on it to see the pic), sweet and soft saulty pastry inherited by the French, or just biscuits or bits of the local 'home' bread.

The Sicilian 'tavola calda' is NOT a 'hot table ...

posted on 18 May 2021

... Tavola Calda is the collective noun grouping many kinds of warm, salty pastries Sicilians just go crazy for.
Pizzette, Arancini, Cartocciate, Raviole alla ricotta, Bombe al forno, are some of the most popular ones and are often available 24/7 in cafes or bars, at the seaside or in the town center. They make an ideal yummy snack as well as a light brunch, lunch or dinner and are so typically Sicilian!

Tavola Calda pastries main ingredients
item topping filling
pizzetta mozzarella cheese, tomato sauce none
arancino none cheese, meat tomato sauce
cartocciata none mozzarella, ham, tomato sauce
raviola none ricotta cheese
bomba none mozzarella, ham
